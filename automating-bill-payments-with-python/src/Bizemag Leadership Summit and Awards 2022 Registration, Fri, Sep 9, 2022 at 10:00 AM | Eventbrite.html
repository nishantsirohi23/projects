About this event

The event is largest conclave of Indian Business Leaders

Bizemag Leadership Summit 2022 brings together hundreds of selected business leaders, entrepreneurs, policy makers, corporate professionals and emerging leaders from the nation who are innovating business ideas and shaping the future.

Join us for over 6 hours of inspired conversation, purposeful networking, and meaningful takeaways.

Under the theme we want, this year’s #Bizemagawards and #Investormeet will take a deep dive on collective and individual evaluation of the entrepreneurial journey; from addressing global innovations in technology, finance and human resources trends, culminating in a Award Felicitation ceremony and a shared vision of the world we all want.
